G-017 — dovere in the future (contracted stem)

Same family as andare/venire — learner request 2026-07-19, same session as G-009.
SituationTarget sentence
"Next week I'll have to finish this project" (io)La settimana prossima dovrò finire questo progetto.
"Tomorrow you'll have to wake up early" (tu)Domani dovrai svegliarti presto.
"In an hour they'll have to leave" (loro)Fra un'ora dovranno partire.
"We'll have to study a lot" (noi)Dovremo studiare molto.
"She'll have to ask permission" (lei)Dovrà chiedere il permesso.
Contrast — andare: "I would go if I had time"Andrei se avessi tempo.
Contrast — venire: "He will come tomorrow"Verrà domani.
Recall the rule: what happens to the stem?It shrinks: dovere→dovr-, andare→andr-, venire→verr- (+ short ending)
Added 2026-07-19 pm (L015): the stem itself (dovr-) held clean all day, but the SAME mood-ending slip (dovremmo for dovremo) hit twice. Different axis from the cards above (ending, not stem):
SituationTarget sentence
"we'll have to leave early" — plain future fact (noi)Domani dovremo partire presto.
Same sentence, softer — "we SHOULD leave early" (not a certainty)Dovremmo partire presto.
Recall: single m or double m — which is which?Dovremo = future, WILL have to (single m) · Dovremmo = conditional, SHOULD (double m)
